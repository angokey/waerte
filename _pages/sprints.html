So you have listened to all the exciting topics on Django for three days and now feel motivated do something about it. What could be a better motivation than contributing to the very open source software you just listened to?

On Thursday and Friday, you have the opportunity to do exactly that. This is your opportunity to contribute to Open Source software. Even if you haven't contributed before, this is also an option to take your first steps. In the sprint, you can contribute to Django projects as you see fit, and if you need help, there will be a pool of experienced contributors to guide you. You can also reach out through Discord in the sprints channel.

Thursday - 10AM to 5PM
Friday - 10AM to 5PM

The Sprints are free for all conference participants. However, in order for us to ensure we have catered for appropriate numbers, please keep your participation status updated on the ticketing website (after buying your ticket).

Read more about getting there on the Venue page.
